ClimateUni

Climateuni is non-profit organization which aims to tackle climate crisis by making Basic Infrastructure and Services (BIS) or resources freely without any cost to local students. Basic Infrastructure and Services(BIS) or resources are Water, Electricity, Health, Internet, Housing, Factory, Education, Security, Transportation and Food.

Covid19

Climateuni is not just limited to tackling Climate change. The resources at Climateuni can be used to tackle any future emergency crisis including current Covid19 pandemic.

Currently Climateuni is providing 3d printed faceshields and straps freely to local hospitals at Kathmandu and Lalitpur.
